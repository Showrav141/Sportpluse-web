About SportPulse

Our Story

Founded in 2025, SportPulse was created by sports enthusiasts who wanted to build a comprehensive platform for fans around the world. We noticed that fans had to visit multiple websites to get scores, news, and merchandise, and we set out to change that.

Today, SportPulse serves millions of users worldwide, providing real-time updates, breaking news, and a trusted marketplace for official sports merchandise.
